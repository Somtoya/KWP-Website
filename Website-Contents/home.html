Who We Are

a collective of artists working to bring our passion for storytelling to film.

Since our founding in 2013,
We have produced eight original short films and five staged productions of our scripts. We work collectively on every aspect of film production, from screenplay to release, and are constantly expanding our resources as we work to create films that are of the highest quality possible. We recently made the move from our home state of Minnesota to Los Angeles and New York City, and the work is only getting stronger. As we continue to grow, trust in Killer Wingfield to always maintain a dedication to creating films that are ambitious, passionate, and new.
